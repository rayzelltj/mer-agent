Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-14

Note: This is a minimal internal-use policy page template. Please review with your legal/compliance owner before relying on it for external distribution.

1. Overview

This integration connects to QuickBooks Online to retrieve accounting reports and data for Monthly End Review (MER) workflows.

2. Data we access

  • QuickBooks Online accounting data and reports that the authorized user permits via Intuit OAuth 2.0.
  • Operational metadata needed to run the integration (authorization tokens, configuration, logs).

3. How we use data

  • To generate bookkeeping review checks (for example, balance sheet reconciliation checks).
  • To support troubleshooting and auditability of review outputs.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with authorized personnel and service providers used to operate the integration, subject to appropriate safeguards.

5. Data retention

Retention depends on your internal policies and system configuration. Tokens and logs should be retained only as long as necessary for operations and compliance.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data. Access is controlled via role-based access and OAuth authorization.

7. Your choices

You may revoke access at any time from within QuickBooks Online by disconnecting the app. After revocation, the integration can no longer access data until re-authorized.

8. Contact

For questions, contact the app administrator or your internal support channel.
